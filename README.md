# International Donk Federation

Official repository for the rules, history, and operations of the Supreme Donk Championships.

The International Donk Federation (IDF) governs organized donk competition worldwide, including the annual Supreme Donk Championships and the Road to Tijuana qualifying circuit.

Competitions evaluate excellence across three disciplines:

• Highest Jump  
• Best in Show  
• Most Supreme  

Select qualifying events are hosted at stadium venues, including parks of Global League Baseball.
